Check out where I've been recently!

Iceland 2024

This is a sponesnuous 10-day road trip! Didn't realize Iceland is so closeby. (~5 hrs from NYC) The scenery was breathtaking. We saw puffins, Arctic foxes, and horses. Surprisingly, the food in Iceland was very nice. I highly recommend their pastries and shellfish soup. Their lamb is also amazing!! 🤤

Tokyo, 2023

My second time in Tokyo but first time seeing Mt. Fuji! I wish I could go back to those days when I was enjoying hot spring on the balcony overseeing the mountain. Did I mention how cute was the capybara cafe and hedgehogs cafe?

Taiwan, 2023

I'm so excited to go back to Taiwan! This is also the first time my husband go visit my hometown and family! It's always nice to be back home. 🥰

California 2022

I ended up traveling to California back-to-back in October and November. In October, I did a 13-hour hike in Idyllwild, and the view was so worth it! In November, I went to Yosemite, Sequoia National Park, and Groveland. Did you spot me in the photo with the Sequoia?
